Why I Built This

Every chain on your block has a website. Your shop deserves one too.

I study engineering and I think about systems. The system I kept seeing in my neighborhood was broken in a specific way: the best businesses were losing customers to worse ones simply because of discoverability.

A customer new to the neighborhood searches "barbershop near me" and clicks the first result with a website. Your shop — with 4.8 stars and a 10-year reputation — does not show up because there is nothing for Google to index. That is a solvable problem.

I built Hidden Gem Digitals to fix that gap one business at a time, starting with the neighborhood I actually live in. Every site I build is designed to put your real reputation front and center — your stars, your reviews, your services, your story.

I am not trying to scale this into a large company. I want to do good work for people who deserve better digital representation, and I want to do it at a price that makes sense for an independent business owner.

Common Questions

Things owners ask me

Do I need to provide anything to get started?

+
Not much. If you have a Google Business listing — which most of you already do — I can pull your photos, reviews, address, and hours directly from there. The main thing I need from you is 30 minutes to talk about what makes your business special so I can write the copy correctly.

What if I want changes after seeing the demo?

+
That is what the demo is for. Every package includes at least one full round of revisions — meaning you can change colors, layout, copy, photos, anything — before the site goes live. I would rather get it exactly right than have you settle for something close.

What does the monthly care plan actually cover?

+
Hosting, SSL certificate, and up to two content updates per month — things like changing your hours, adding a new service, updating photos, or swapping out a staff member. It also includes keeping your Google Business profile synced with your site. If something breaks, I fix it the same day. No ticket system, no call center — you text me directly.

How long does the whole thing take?

+
From our first conversation to a live website is typically 10 to 14 days. The demo usually takes 48 hours after our call. Revisions add a few days depending on how much you want changed. If you have a specific date in mind — a grand reopening, a seasonal push, a special event — tell me and I will prioritize accordingly.

Will the website show up on Google?

+
Every site I build is optimized for local search from day one — correct meta tags, fast load times, mobile-first code, and a Google Business integration that links your site back to your Maps listing. You will not rank on page one overnight, but within 4 to 8 weeks of launch most businesses see meaningful improvement in how they appear in local searches.

What happens if you graduate and leave?

+
Fair question. Every site is built on standard code that any web developer can pick up. You own the domain and the hosting account — not me. If I ever stopped doing this, you would have a fully functional website that another developer could maintain without starting from scratch. I will also give you a full handover document explaining exactly how everything works.
